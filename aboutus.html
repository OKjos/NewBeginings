Meet Our Pastor

Todd Meece was called to serve New Beginnings Ministries, in the summer of 2024. He has a love for God's people, and a desire to see the lost come to know Christ. Pastor Todd considers his preaching style to be "theologically practical", and hopes that the folks who come to NBM will have a deep love for God, and His Word. Todd has four children and five grandchildren. He holds degrees from Purdue University, Crown College, and Andersonville Theological Seminary.

At NBM we strive to reach out to others who have never heard the Gospel message. We are here to help you get through difficult times, accomplish your goals and surround you with caring people who will give you positive encouragement and support you in any way we can.
